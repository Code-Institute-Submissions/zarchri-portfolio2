Fun facts
    Part of the reason Disney's movies were so successful was that the animators were amazing dedicated to getting things right. To make the cuddly characters in his films as realistic as possible, he often took in live animals in the studio. During the production for Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs was taken live rabbits, skunks and horses in for the animators to study. Likewise, two fawns roamed around during the production of Bambi
